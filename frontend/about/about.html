❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using SmartCity for efficient complaint management.

How do I track my complaint?

Navigate to the Track Status page in the Citizen Portal. Enter your unique complaint ID to view the complete timeline, current status, assigned department/staff, and all progress updates. The page auto-refreshes every 30 seconds to show real-time changes.

Who assigns my complaint?

SmartCity administrators review each complaint and intelligently route it to the appropriate department and staff member based on the issue category, geographic zone, and current workload distribution. You'll receive notification once assignment is complete.

Can I update my information?

Yes. Access your profile from the Citizen Portal to update contact details, view complaint history, and manage your account settings. All changes are saved securely and take effect immediately.

What statuses exist and what do they mean?

Pending: Complaint received, awaiting assignment. In Progress: Actively being worked on. Resolved: Issue fixed and closed. Rejected: Doesn't meet criteria or duplicate. Each transition is timestamped.

How does the system ensure accountability?

Every action is logged with timestamps, user IDs, and assignment history. Administrators can track staff performance, resolution times, and departmental efficiency through comprehensive analytics and audit trails.
